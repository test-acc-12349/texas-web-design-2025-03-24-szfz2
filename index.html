Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical website development timeline?

Most projects are completed within 4-6 weeks, depending on complexity. We follow a structured process including discovery, design, development, testing, and launch phases to ensure quality delivery.

What hosting and maintenance services do you provide?

We offer premium hosting with 99.9% uptime guarantee, daily backups, SSL certificates, and 24/7 security monitoring. Our maintenance plans include regular updates, performance optimization, and technical support.

Every website we build is fully responsive and tested across all devices and browsers. We use a mobile-first approach to ensure optimal performance on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support including technical assistance, content updates, and performance monitoring. Our support team is available during business hours with emergency support available 24/7.
